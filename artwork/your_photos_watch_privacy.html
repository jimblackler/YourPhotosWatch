Your Photos Watch privacy policy

Your Photos Watch is an Android Wear application By Jim Blackler. Enquiries welcome at jimblackler@gmail.com

Privacy policy

Information and content you supply is only stored within the application on the phone and watch where it is installed ('your devices').

The application only collects and stores information and content necessary to perform its advertised functionality.

Communication to third parties is limited to the minimum required to download your photos from the specific services you select. Only the photos you explicitly select are stored on your devices.
